      <h2 align="center">Client Background</h2>
      <body>
        Kinetiq is a global electronics company that sells consumer electronics to a global clientele. Established in 2016, the company has seen small but steady growth in its  
        first few years of operation leading up to the COVID-19 pandemic, followed by a dramatic pre-pandemic surge in late 2019 to early 2020. However, this peak was immediately met           by a sharp decline as the pandemic arrived. <br> 
        <br>
        Kinetiq’s book of business has just surpassed 15,000 customers and is approaching 200,000 items sold, generating sales revenue exceeding $55 million. The retailer’s available           data spans various metrics and dimensions, including sales, customers, products, and stores. <br>
        <br>
        This analysis was conducted to support leadership decision-making around profitability, channel strategy, and customer focus. This in-depth review provides valuable insights            that cross-functional teams will utilize to enhance Kinetiq’s performance. The key insights and recommendations focus on the following areas: <br>
        <br>
      </body>
      <h3>Northstar Metrics:</h3> 
      <h4>
        <ul><li>Profit trends - Isolating the focus on profit over time.</li>
          <li>Product category perfromance - Analyzing product categories driving profit, market impact, and how the trend have changed.</li>
          <li>Customer demographics - Focusing on profit distribution within customer age groups and location.</li>
          <li>Online vs. in-store geographic segmentation - Analyzing profit on the country and state level, and identifying profit drivers by store sie and efficiency.</li>
